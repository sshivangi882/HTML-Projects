Red Velvat Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 cups (360g) cake flour (spooned & leveled)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 Tablespoons (10g) unsweetened natural cocoa
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) canola or vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature and separated
  • 1 Tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
  • liquid or gel red food coloring
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk, at room temperature

Cream Cheese Frosting

16 ounces (450g) full-fat block cream cheese, softened to room temperature
3/4 cup (170g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
5 cups (600g) confectionersâ€™ sugar
1 and 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
pinch of salt, to taste

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the vegetable oil, buttermilk, eggs, red food coloring, vanilla extract, and white vinegar.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined and smooth.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the cakes have cooled, frost them with cream cheese frosting. You can also frost the sides if desired.

  9. Slice and serve the Red Velvet Cake. Enjoy!
