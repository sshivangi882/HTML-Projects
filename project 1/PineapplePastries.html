Pineapple Pastries

Ingredients

    For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup cold water

    • For the pineapple filling:

  • 2 cups pineapple chunks, drained and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • For the glaze:

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk

Notes on Ingredients

You can also sprinkle some powdered sugar on top of the pastries for an extra touch of sweetness and decoration. Store the pastries in an airtight container.

Method

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and chilled, cubed butter. Use your fingertips or a pastry cutter to rub the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the cold water, a little at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
  3. Shape the dough into a ball, cover it with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. While the dough chills, prepare the pineapple filling. In a saucepan, combine the pineapple chunks, granulated sugar, water, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and becomes glossy.
  5. Remove the pineapple filling from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow it to cool completely.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  7. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Cut out small circles or squares using a cookie cutter or a knife.
  8. Place a spoonful of the pineapple filling in the center of each pastry piece.
  9. Fold the pastry over the filling, pressing the edges together to seal. You can use a fork to create a decorative pattern along the edges.
  10. Place the filled pastries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the pastries are golden brown.
  12. While the pastries cool, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar and milk until smooth and pourable.
  13. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled pastries.
  14. Serve and enjoy the delightful pineapple pastries!
